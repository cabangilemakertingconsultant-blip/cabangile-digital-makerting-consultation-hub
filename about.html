About Cabangile Hub

Our Story

Built on Passion, Driven by Results

Founded in 2016, Cabangile Marketing Consultant Hub was born from a simple belief — that every South African business, regardless of size, deserves access to world-class marketing expertise.

What started as a one-woman consultancy has grown into a full-service marketing powerhouse with a team of 20+ specialists serving clients across South Africa and beyond. Our approach blends African creativity with global marketing intelligence.

Today, we are proud to have helped over 200 businesses transform their brands, grow their revenues, and build lasting relationships with their customers.
